About SKYTOP College

Skytop College is a leading educational institution committed to nurturing excellence, building strong character, and developing the full potential of every child. Since its establishment, it has remained dedicated to providing a **safe, engaging, and academically rich environment** where students can grow intellectually, socially, and morally.

At Sky Top School, we believe that every child can succeed when given the right support, guidance, and opportunities. Our experienced teachers use **modern teaching methods** to make learning enjoyable, practical, and impactful. We blend strong academics with moral instruction, leadership training, and extracurricular activities that help students discover and develop their talents.

Our Commitment

We are committed to raising well-rounded learners who can confidently face the challenges of tomorrow. At Sky Top School, every child is valued, guided, and encouraged to reach for the skies.

Our History

SKYTOP College has a very rich History which began in **September 2006**.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that inspires students to achieve academic excellence, build confidence, and become responsible future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a model school in Igbogbo and beyond—known for discipline, innovation, and the holistic development of every child.
